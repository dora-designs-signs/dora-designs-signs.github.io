Laser Cutting / Engraving

We can laser cut your design, artwork or product!

What can we cut?

The material type determines what laser will be required to cut your part. We have both fiber & CO2 lasers to cut nearly all materials - if it can be cut, we can cut it!

  1. Plastics (Acrylic, Polycarbonate, ABS, Delrin, HDPE, PVC, Starboard, Foams, etc.)
  2. Wood (Softwoods, Hardwoods, MDF, Bamboo, Laminates, etc.)
  3. Soft Metals (Aluminum, Brass, Copper)
  4. Composites (Fiberglass, Carbon fiber, CFRPs, etc.)

We can laser cut sheets of:

  1. Plastics (Acrylic, ABS, some Foams, Polyurethane, etc)
  2. Wood (Softwoods, Hardwoods, MDF, Bamboo)
  3. Organics (Leather, Cork, Paper, Rubber, Fabrics, etc)
  4. Metals (carbon steel, stainless steel, copper, brass, aluminum and titanium)

We can also add engravings, markings or burning to an existing product. Add a permanent logo, artwork, lasered image, or special message for a life event. Phone cases, knives, Ipads, cutting boards, glasses, just to name a few.
