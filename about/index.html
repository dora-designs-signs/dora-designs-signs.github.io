About

Dora Designs is located in beautiful Mount Dora, just outside of Orlando, FL.

We service all of Central Florida, including: Sanford, Lake Mary, Apopka, Oviedo, Winter Park, Maitland, Altamonte, Ocoee, Winter Garden, Clermont, Minneola, Leesburg, Tavares, and The Villages.

Capabilities/equipment

Our current equipment includes:

  1. 4’x8’ 3-axis CNC router dual spindle with 8 tool ATC
  2. 24"x20"x20" CNC Mill
  3. 22"x16"x18" CNC Mill w/ 4th axis
  4. 5’x10’ FIBER CNC Laser
  5. 4’x3’ CO2 CNC Laser
  6. Miller 325 engine-driven welder for mobile fabrication, repairs & backup power generation
  7. multiple 3D Printers for rapid prototyping, molds and samples
  8. supporting manual cutting/drilling/pressing/sanding and QC inspection equipment

About the Owner

Owner/operator Jon Waldmann started Dora Designs and Signs in May 2020 in pursuit of a fullfilling, hands-on career merging his technical talents and creative desires. Jon graduated from UCF with a BS in Mechanical Engineering and spent 7 years in medical product design and CNC manufacturing.

With the global spread of COVID-19, Jon put his design and manufacturing talents to work creating custom fit and aesthetically pleasing sneeze guards for professional offices with unique features to meet individual process flow needs.

Continued investment in the business in 2022 brought industrial CNC milling machines, upgraded cnc router, advanced softwares, and more support equipment to broaden the capabilities of his shop.
