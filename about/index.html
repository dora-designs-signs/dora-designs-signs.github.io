About

Dora Designs is located in beautiful Mount Dora, Fl. just outside of Orlando. We currently operate in a climate controlled 800sq.ft. space with room to grow!

We service all of Central Florida, including: Sanford, Lake Mary, Apopka, Oviedo, Winter Park, Maitland, Altamonte, Ocoee, Winter Garden, Clermont, Minneola, Leesburg, Tavares, and The Villages.

Capabilities/equipment

Our current equipment includes:

  1. 4’x8’ 3-axis CNC router (3hp ER20 spindle, air blast & coolant mist, external dust collection)
  2. 4’x3’ 2.5-axis CNC Laser (150W CO2, chuck & roller rotary attachments, external air extraction, 4 sided pass thru’s to handle larger workpieces)
  3. Trailblazer 325 engine-driven welder for fabrication, repairs & backup power generation
  4. 7’x12’ Flatbed Truck for easy pickup and delivery of larger product
  5. 1400lb forklift for loading/unloading large workpieces and material pallets
  6. supporting manual cutting/drilling/sanding and QC inspection equipment
  7. COMING SOON! - 4x4’ Thermoforming table

We can machine/carve any size upto a full 4X8 sheets (upto 7.5” thick) of:

  1. Plastics (Acrylic, Polycarbonate, ABS, Delrin, HDPE, PVC, Starboard, Foams, etc.)
  2. Wood (Softwoods, Hardwoods, MDF, Bamboo, Laminates, etc.)
  3. Soft Metals (Aluminum, Brass, Copper)
  4. Composites (Fiberglass, Carbonfiber, CFRPs, etc.)

We can laser cut sheets of:

  1. Plastics (Acrylic, ABS, some Foams, Polyurethane, etc)
  2. Wood (Softwoods, Hardwoods, MDF, Bamboo)
  3. Organics (Leather, Cork, Paper, Rubber, Fabrics, etc)
  4. steel shim stock <0.01” thickness (<0.25mm)

In addition, we can laser etch/mark:

  1. Anything listed above
  2. Metals (Anodized Aluminum, Carbon Steel, Stainless Steels)
  3. Glass & mirrors

About the Owner

Owner/operator Jon Waldmann started Dora Designs and Signs in May 2020 in pursuit of a fullfilling, hands-on career merging his technical talents and creative desires. Jon graduated from the UCF with a BS in Mechanical Engineering and spent 7 years in medical product design and CNC manufacturing.

With the global spread of COVID-19, Jon put his design and manufacturing talents to work creating custom fit and aesthetically pleasing sneeze guards for professional offices with unique features to meet individual process flow needs.
