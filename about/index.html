About

Waldmann Enterprises is located in beautiful Mount Dora, Florida, in the greater Orlando area.

About the Owner

Owner/operator Jon Waldmann started Waldmann Enterprises in 2020 in pursuit of a fullfilling, hands-on, career merging his technical talents and creative desires. Jon graduated from UCF with a BS in Mechanical Engineering and spent 7 years in medical product design and CNC manufacturing. As a gear-head with a black belt and a passion for the outdoors, he is not afraid of challenges that may require developing unconventional methods to achieve desired output.

Starting a business in the height of the COVID pandemic, Jon put his design and manufacturing talents to work creating custom fit and aesthetically pleasing sneeze guards for professional offices with unique features to meet individual process flow needs.

Continued investment in the business brought industrial CNC milling machines, advanced softwares, 3D-Printers, lasers and more support equipment to broaden the capabilities of his shop.

We look forward to future opportunities and partnerships with companies that value loyalty, quality and continued product & process improvement.

We service all of Central Florida, including: Sanford, Lake Mary, Apopka, Oviedo, Winter Park, Maitland, Altamonte, Ocoee, Winter Garden, Clermont, Minneola, Leesburg, Tavares, and The Villages. We are eager to expand our reach, as we begin to work with manufacturers on the space coast from Daytona to Melbourne; on the Gulf Coast in Tampa, Clearwater and Port Richey; and throughout the High Tech Corridor with researchers in Gainesville, down to Lake Nona.
