What can we cut?

The material and size determines what process will be best suited for your application. We can machine parts ranging from millimeter-sized to 4x8 ft. Some common materials are listed below:

  1. Non-ferrous Soft Metals (Aluminum, Brass, Copper)
  2. Hard metals (Carbon steels, stainless steels, tool steels, cast iron, titanium, etc)
  3. Plastics (Acrylic, Polycarbonate, ABS, Nylon, Delrin, HDPE, PVC, etc.)
  4. Wood (Softwoods, Hardwoods, MDF, butcher blocks, milled slabs, Laminates, etc.)
  5. Composites (Fiberglass, Carbon fiber, CFRPs, etc.)
  6. Others (Neoprene, rubber, leather, gasket material, foam, etc)
  7. Modification of existing machine parts or tools
